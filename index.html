Reservations

North Beach Plantation: The room rates listed above are on a space available basis. We recommend you visit their website (click on the image above) and research the room types prior to calling to reserve your rooms. After navigating to the hotel's website, click on 'Accommodations' on the left hand side to view the above listed room names so you can see which is best for you. When making your reservation, you must identify yourselves with the Gore Rogers Wedding in order to ensure you receive the special disounted rate for the group. All reservations must be received by May 10, 2016 in order to receive this special discounted rate. Check-in time is 4pm. Check-out time is 11am.

Beach Cove Resort: Each individual is responsible for making your own reservations by calling the Beach Cove Resort at 1-800-331-6533, press option 2 and refer to the Gore Rogers Wedding in order to ensure you receive the special disounted rate for the group. All reservations must be received by May 10, 2016 in order to receive this special discounted rate. Check-out time is 11am. Late Check-out: If you check out past 11am, you will be charged a half day's rate until 12pm. Thereafter you will be charged a full day's rate of room and tax.

Attire

Beach Formal: So dress to impress, but dress for the elements (sun, sand, and water). Anything you'd wear to a nice restaurant on a summer day is appropriate.

He should wear: A summer suit with linen shirt (no ties required), linen pants or khakis, and sandals or dress shoes.

She should wear: A formal sundress at tea or knee length with flat sandals or heels. Make-up and hair can be natural and every day.
