Reservations

North Beach Plantation: The room rates listed above are on a space available basis. We recommend you visit their website (click on the image above) and research the room types prior to calling to reserve your rooms.

After navigating to the hotel's website, click on 'Accommodations' on the left hand side to view the above listed room names so you can see which is best for you.

Each individual is responsible for making your own reservations by calling the hotel at 1-855-842-6558.

When making your reservation, you must identify yourselves with the Gore Rogers Wedding in order to ensure you receive the special disounted rate for the group. All reservations must be received by May 10, 2016 in order to receive this special discounted rate. Check-in time is 4pm. Check-out time is 11am.

Beach Cove Resort: Each individual is responsible for making your own reservations by calling the Beach Cove Resort at 1-800-331-6533, press option 2 and refer to the Gore Rogers Wedding in order to ensure you receive the special disounted rate for the group.

All reservations must be received by May 10, 2016 in order to receive this special discounted rate. Check-out time is 11am.

LATE CHECK-OUT: If you check out past 11am, you will be charged a half day's rate until 12pm. Thereafter you will be charged a full day's rate of room and tax.

Attire

Beach Formal: So dress to impress, but dress for the elements (sun, sand, and water). Anything you'd wear to a nice restaurant on a summer day is appropriate.

He should wear: A summer suit with linen shirt (no ties required), linen pants or khakis, and sandals or dress shoes.

She should wear: A formal sundress at tea or knee length with flat sandals or heels. Make-up and hair can be natural and every day.

Our Story

We met at a lounge in downtown Cincinnati. We had such an enjoyable time that at the end of the evening we exchanged phone numbers. Brent called Tierra thirty minutes later to make sure she made it home safely. Seven years later, we are inseparable.


Last May, Brent took Tierra on the premier fly fishing river in northern Wisconsin and stopped at a secret family picnic ground where Brent has been picnicking since a little boy. Brent got on one knee and asked for Tierra's hand in marriage. She said Yes!


We are so excited to get married and share our lives together.

Hello family and friends!

We are so happy and excited to share our special day with you! We have created this website to keep everyone updated with the information that will be useful while we plan our wedding. We have people scattered all over the country, and we know you all have busy lives. It means so much to have all of our family and friends loving and supporting us. Can't wait to see you all in June!
